Responsible Social Media Use Checklist for College Students
 
 Do’s
Think before you post – Ensure your content is appropriate and respectful.
Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal details.
Verify information before sharing – Check facts to prevent spreading misinformation.
Maintain a positive digital footprint – Share content that reflects your values and goals.
Engage respectfully – Be polite and considerate in comments and discussions.
 Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive personal information – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling – Respect others’ opinions and differences.
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content – It can harm your reputation and future opportunities.
Don’t believe or spread fake news – Always cross-check sources.
Don’t overuse social media – Excessive use can affect productivity and mental health.
