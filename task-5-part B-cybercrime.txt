“Stay Safe Online” Checklist for College Students (India)
 
    Prevention Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, and symbols).
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
Verify links before clicking—check URLs carefully, especially in emails or messages.
Limit personal information online—don’t overshare your phone number, address, or college details.
Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public computers.
Keep your device updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
 
   Financial / UPI Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve “collect requests” without verifying the sender.
Double-check payment details (name and UPI ID) before sending money.
   
  Reporting & Help
Report cyber fraud immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud assistance
